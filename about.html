About Our Foundation

Creating Opportunities Transforming Lives

NayePankh Foundation is dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, skill development and social welfare programs. We believe that every individual deserves equal opportunities to learn, grow and build a brighter future.

Our Journey Join Us
OUR STORY

A Journey Of Hope, Growth & Impact

Founded in 2018, NayePankh Foundation began with a simple vision — to provide every child with access to quality education and opportunities. Over the years, we have expanded our efforts to healthcare, digital literacy, women empowerment and community development programs.

Today, our dedicated volunteers and partners continue working towards a future where every individual can realize their full potential.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones That Shaped Our Mission

Every year brought new opportunities to serve, empower and create impact.

2018

Foundation Established

NayePankh Foundation was launched with the mission of supporting children through education and social welfare programs.

2020

1000+ Children Supported

Scholarships, school kits and mentoring programs helped students continue their education during challenging times.

2022

Expansion To Rural Communities

Programs expanded to more than 80 villages focusing on education, healthcare and women empowerment.

2024

Digital Literacy Initiative

Computer training and digital awareness sessions were introduced to equip youth with modern skills.

2026

5000+ Lives Impacted

Today, NayePankh continues to transform lives through sustainable community development initiatives.
